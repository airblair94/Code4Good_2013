Vision and Mission

Founded in 1977 by a group of community residents, Milford Housing Development Corporation(MHDC) is a value-driven housing developer, offering a sensible approach in a caring environment, and providing high-quality affordable housing solutions. With a full-continuum of housing programs, MHDC has steadily grown into Delaware's leading nonprofit provider of affordable housing.
